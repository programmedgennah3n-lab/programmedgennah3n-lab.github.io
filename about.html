About Us

Village Distribution Limited is a registered limited company based in Rural Area 25, Lilongwe, Malawi. We improve access to essential goods and services in rural communities while building a sustainable local business network.

We source fast-moving consumer goods, household items, and selected agricultural products from wholesalers and distribute them to rural areas, reducing costs and ensuring availability.

Our team includes local community members as sales agents and distribution support staff, creating employment and encouraging local entrepreneurship.
